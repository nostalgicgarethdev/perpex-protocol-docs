Architecture

Perpex is a Next.js application backed by Neon Postgres and a dedicated matching engine for mark prices, funding, and liquidations.

Frontend

Three surfaces powered by the Next.js App Router:

  • Marketing at / — hero, mechanics, live board, CTA
  • Launchpad board at /launchpad — sortable market list with positions and history
  • Trading terminal at /launchpad/[symbol] — chart, order ticket, open positions

Database

Five Postgres tables managed via scripts/migrate.mjs:

TablePurpose
positionsOpen and closed positions with TP/SL and liquidation price
ordersResting limit and stop orders
user_marketsMarkets created via the launchpad
demo_balancesPer-wallet demo USDC balance
demo_depositsAppend-only ledger of on-chain top-ups

Matching engine

The Perpex engine handles:

  • Mark price — TWAP-blended price stream per market
  • Bonding-curve liquidity — virtual pool for newborn markets
  • Funding — periodic long-vs-short imbalance settlement
  • Liquidations — sub-second monitoring loop
  • Order triggers — limit, stop, TP/SL execution

Cross-market safety

A position opened on market A must never be settled against market B's mark. Every loop that touches mark price filters by position.token_symbol === currentSymbol before doing anything destructive.
