Launch a market

Anyone can create a perpetual futures market on Perpex. Paste an SPL token mint, configure leverage and fees, pay the launch fee — and the market is live on the board.

Overview

Market creation is a single-signature flow at /launchpad/create. There is no listing committee, no application form, and no waiting period. The creator wallet becomes the permanent fee recipient for that market.

Launch steps

  1. Connect your Solana wallet
  2. Search for a token by symbol or paste its mint address
  3. Set max leverage (2× – 100×) and trading fee (3 – 100 bps per side)
  4. Pay the 0.3 SOL launch fee
  5. Market appears on the launchpad board before the next block confirms

Market lifecycle

Every market passes through three states:

01

Seeded

Virtual liquidity from the bonding curve. Trades clear against the curve until graduation depth is reached.

02

Graduated

Real depth from passive LPs kicks in. The curve becomes a soft fallback, no longer the primary venue.

03

Retired

If 24h volume sits below floor for 30 days, the market enters wind-down. Open positions can close; new ones are blocked.

Bonding curve liquidity

Newborn markets open against a virtual constant-product pool sized to the chosen leverage cap:

x × y = k

Graduation rule:  cumulative_real_volume ≥ $50,000
Initial depth:    depth = base_depth × (max_leverage / 25)

On graduation, real-LP liquidity takes over as the primary venue. Creators are paid out of every fill — virtual or real.

Creator addresses

Creator addresses are non-transferable in v0.1. If you launch a market, you and only you collect on it. Multi-sig creator wallets are on the roadmap.
