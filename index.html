About Perpex

Perpex is a permissionless perpetuals protocol on Solana — built to let anyone launch a leveraged market for any token that trades, without a listing committee, whitelist, or waiting room.

Perpex is part of a broader mission to democratize access to derivatives markets. It is designed to enable seamless long and short exposure to any SPL token, with up to 100× isolated leverage, self-custodied wallets, and markets that go live the moment a launch fee clears.

Built for any token

At its core, Perpex is optimized for permissionless market creation — memecoins, DeFi tokens, xStocks, and everything in between. If a token has a price feed, it can have a perp. Markets open with virtual liquidity from a bonding curve, so spreads are tight from minute one.

Built on proven technology

The stack leverages Next.js 16, Neon Postgres, and a dedicated Perpex matching engine for mark prices, funding, liquidations, and bonding-curve liquidity. Price data flows through Birdeye; on-chain deposits settle via Solana mainnet.

Open and permissionless

Perpex is designed to be permissionless and developer-friendly. Anyone can connect a wallet, trade existing markets, or launch new ones. The protocol does not gatekeep listings — creators earn 10% of every trading fee for the lifetime of their market.

Beta live on Solana

Perpex is currently in beta on Solana mainnet. Connect through the details on the getting started page, explore markets on the launchpad, or read the source on GitHub.
