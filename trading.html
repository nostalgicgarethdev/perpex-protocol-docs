Trading guide

Six order types, isolated margin, and real-time liquidation monitoring — available on every Perpex market from day one.

Quickstart

  1. Connect a Solana wallet at /launchpad
  2. Select a market from the board or search by symbol
  3. Choose long or short, set collateral and leverage
  4. Pick an order type and review your projected liquidation price
  5. Sign once — your position opens immediately

Beta notice

Stop and take-profit orders are evaluated client-side during beta — they only fire while the terminal tab is open. A server-side keeper network is the next major release.

Risk & liquidation

Every position is isolated. Margin and PnL are computed against a single position; losses on one market can never spill into another. If margin falls below the maintenance requirement, the position is liquidated.

TierInitial marginMaintenance margin
≤ 10×10%5%
≤ 25×4%2%
≤ 50×2%1%
≤ 100×1%0.5%

Liquidations are processed by the protocol keeper. A 0.5% liquidation fee is paid from remaining collateral: half to the keeper, half to the insurance fund.

FAQ

What about MEV?

Order placement is private until inclusion. Market orders settle against the curve, so there is no orderbook for searchers to sandwich in v0.1. Limit orders are batched on settle.

What if the price feed goes stale?

If the price feed becomes stale beyond 60 seconds, the market enters protective mode: only reduce-only orders are accepted until a feed is restored or the market is retired.

Why Solana?

Sub-second blocks and fees small enough that the keeper-driven liquidation model is actually viable. The trading engine assumes both.
